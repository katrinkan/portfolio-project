About me

<my path>

My way to coding was everything but straight. Growing up with a super tech-loving dad, having access to a computer since I can think, beeing able to go online since the early days of the internet and of course having an iPhone since day one, were the most natural things to me. So natural, that I actually never really gave it a thougth. It was just there.
As a child, I soon discovered that I love to build things. That love never died. Making a plan, figuring out what it needs, build it, test it, retry and finally seeing a result is just pure magic to me.

I later became a veggie garderer working at a CSA. My focus lies on plant production, seed-keeping and plant breeding. Again, a lot of building, testing and retrying!
By the end of 2020, my husband Philipp and I moved to the coutryside. We are now living in a small, idyllic, subalpine village with a spectacular view on the mointains.
Coding was on my "Things I really want to learn"-list for some time already, but in November 2021 I finally signed up for a workshop at SheCodes. I soon figured out, that basically, coding is just what I love - it's building stuff!

Let's build something together!
